# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the gokuai yunku Datasource Plugin for Dify.

## Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. All data processed by the plugin is handled locally or through secure connections to gokuai yunku, as required for functionality.

## Use of Information

The plugin only accesses your gokuai yunku objects to provide the requested features. No data is stored or transmitted to third parties by the plugin itself.

## Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your data. All communications with Cloudflare R2 services use secure protocols (HTTPS) and Cloudflare's secure authentication mechanisms. We do not retain any user credentials or file data beyond the duration of your session.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated effective date.
